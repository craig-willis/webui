Note:

We are working on a workflow that will let us publish the generated indexes here. We want to make sure we have a proper versioning, citation and attribution process in place.

The indexes will be generated with the metadata of our partners who provide the data to the community and P418. We want to ensure that the approach properly cites and provides provenance to the data.

When ready, you will be able to access and download the same indexes this site and the associated web services use. The goal is to have others in the community contribute to the tasks of interfacing the material provided by the facilities with the research community.

Our goal is to have this material here and ready before the June EarthCube All Hands meeting.
